## What a good SEO agency actually does

SEO isn't one thing. It's a collection of practices that, done together, improve your visibility on search engines. A competent agency covers all of these:

### Technical SEO

This is the foundation. Your website needs to be technically sound before any other SEO work matters.

- **Site speed** — Pages should load in under 3 seconds on mobile. Google directly penalises slow sites.
- **Mobile responsiveness** — Over 70% of searches in India happen on phones. Your site must work flawlessly on mobile.
- **Crawlability** — Google's bots need to be able to find and read all your important pages. This requires proper sitemap submission, robots.txt configuration, and clean URL structure.
- **Core Web Vitals** — Google's specific performance metrics (Largest Contentful Paint, First Input Delay, Cumulative Layout Shift) directly affect rankings.
- **HTTPS** — Your site must have an SSL certificate. Non-HTTPS sites are flagged as "Not Secure" and ranked lower.

### On-page SEO

This is optimising the content on your actual pages.

- **Keyword research** — Identifying what terms your potential customers are searching for, and how competitive those terms are
- **Title tags and meta descriptions** — The text that appears in Google search results. These need to be compelling and include target keywords.
- **Heading structure** — Proper use of H1, H2, H3 tags that organise content logically
- **Content optimisation** — Writing content that genuinely answers what searchers are looking for, with natural keyword integration
- **Internal linking** — Connecting your pages together in a logical structure that helps both users and Google understand your site

### Off-page SEO

This is building your website's authority through external signals.

- **Backlinks** — Getting other reputable websites to link to yours. Quality matters far more than quantity.
- **Local citations** — Ensuring your business information is consistent across Google Business Profile, JustDial, IndiaMART, Sulekha, and other directories
- **Google Business Profile optimisation** — For local businesses, this is critical for appearing in map results

### Content strategy

- **Blog content** — Creating useful, informative content that targets keywords your potential customers are searching for
- **Content calendar** — A planned approach to publishing, not random posts when someone remembers
- **Content updating** — Refreshing older content to keep it relevant and competitive

## 7 signs of a good SEO agency

### 1. They show you their own rankings

A good SEO agency ranks well for their own keywords. Search "SEO agency Jamshedpur" or "digital marketing Jamshedpur" — if they don't appear on page 1 for their own industry terms, why would you trust them to rank your business?

### 2. They do a proper audit before proposing anything

Before telling you what they'll do, a good agency analyses what you currently have. They should audit your website's technical health, current keyword rankings, competitor landscape, and content gaps — then build a strategy based on actual data.

An agency that proposes a generic SEO package without looking at your specific situation is not doing SEO — they're selling a template.

### 3. They explain their strategy in plain language

SEO can get technical, but a good agency can explain what they're doing and why in terms you understand. If they hide behind jargon and can't explain how their work translates to more customers for your business, that's a problem.

### 4. They set realistic timelines

SEO takes time. A good agency will tell you that meaningful results typically take 3–6 months, with significant improvements at 6–12 months. Anyone promising first-page rankings in 30 days is either lying or using tactics that will eventually get your site penalised by Google.

### 5. They focus on traffic that converts, not just traffic

10,000 visitors who don't buy anything are worth less than 100 visitors who become customers. A good SEO agency focuses on targeting keywords with commercial intent — terms that indicate someone is ready to hire or purchase — not just high-volume keywords that bring irrelevant traffic.

### 6. They provide transparent reporting

You should receive regular reports (monthly at minimum) that clearly show:
- Which keywords you're ranking for and how positions have changed
- Organic traffic trends
- Which pages are getting the most traffic
- Lead or enquiry numbers from organic search
- What work was done that month

If you're paying for SEO and can't see clear data on what's happening, you're flying blind.

### 7. They don't guarantee #1 rankings

This is perhaps the most important sign. No legitimate SEO professional guarantees specific rankings. Google's algorithm considers hundreds of factors, many of which are outside any agency's control. An agency that guarantees #1 is either dishonest or planning to use manipulative tactics.

What a good agency will guarantee: consistent effort, transparent reporting, and a strategy built on best practices that Google rewards.

## Red flags when evaluating SEO agencies

**They promise instant results.** SEO doesn't work that way. Period.

**They won't share their methods.** If an agency is secretive about what they actually do, they may be using black-hat techniques like buying backlinks from link farms, keyword stuffing, or private blog networks. These can get your site penalised or removed from Google entirely.

**Their pricing is suspiciously cheap.** Quality SEO requires significant time and expertise. If an agency offers "full SEO" for ₹3,000/month, they're either doing almost nothing, outsourcing to someone who does almost nothing, or using automated tools that Google's algorithm has long since learned to detect and penalise.

**They focus only on rankings, not business outcomes.** Rankings are a means to an end. The end is more customers and revenue. An agency that only talks about keyword positions without connecting them to business growth is missing the point.

**They lock you into long contracts without a trial period.** A confident agency will offer a 3-month trial or at least a month-to-month option. Agencies that demand 12-month contracts upfront often do so because they know clients would leave once they see the lack of results.

**They don't have case studies or references.** Any established SEO agency should be able to show you real examples of businesses they've helped rank — with before and after data. Ask for references and actually call them.
