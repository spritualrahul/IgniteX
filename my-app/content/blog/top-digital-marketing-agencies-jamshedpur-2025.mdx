## What makes a good digital marketing agency?

Before the list, let's define what "good" actually means in this context. A strong digital marketing agency should:

- **Have a proven track record** with real, verifiable client results
- **Offer a clear strategy**, not just random social media posts or ad campaigns
- **Understand your local market** — Jamshedpur is not Mumbai, and the approach needs to reflect that
- **Be transparent about pricing** and what's included
- **Communicate regularly** with progress reports and data, not just vague updates
- **Focus on ROI**, not vanity metrics like followers or likes

## Questions to ask any agency before hiring

Regardless of which agency you choose, ask these questions before signing anything:

1. **Can you show me live examples of work you've done for similar businesses?**
2. **What specific results did you achieve for those clients?** (Ask for numbers — traffic, leads, revenue, not just "increased engagement")
3. **What's included in the price and what costs extra?**
4. **Who will actually work on my account?** (Not the salesperson — the person doing the work)
5. **How will you report progress and how often?**
6. **What's the minimum contract period?**
7. **What happens if I'm not happy with the results?**

A good agency will answer all of these confidently and honestly. An agency that gets defensive or vague is one you should avoid.
