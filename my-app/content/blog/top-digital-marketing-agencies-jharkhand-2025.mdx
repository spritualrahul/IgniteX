## Before you hire: questions to ask every agency

1. **Can you show me 3–5 live projects with measurable results?** Not screenshots — actual live websites or campaign data.
2. **Who will work on my account?** Meet the actual team, not just the sales person.
3. **What's your reporting process?** You should get clear, data-driven monthly reports.
4. **What's the minimum contract period?** Avoid agencies that lock you into 12-month contracts without a trial.
5. **What happens if I'm not satisfied?** Understand exit terms before you sign.
